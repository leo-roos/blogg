Glass Med Rostad Lök

This is a very unusual dessert made with roasted onions. It might sound strange, but the caramelized flavor actually works surprisingly well.

Some chefs even describe it as a sweet and savory fusion. In older recipes you may also see ingredients that were removed replaced with better alternatives.

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Roasted onions
  • Vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Slice the onions thinly
  2. Roast them until caramelized
  3. Mix with milk and sugar
  4. Freeze for 3 hours

Chef's Tip

Use sweet onions for the best flavor. Yellow onions will work, but sweet onions caramelize better.
